Absolutely Ultimate Potato Soup

Description

Potato soup is very tasty on a cold fall or winter day. This recipe is very simple to make and a guaranteed crowd pleaser. Pair with a grilled cheese sandwich and you will have yourself a nice meal of soup and sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cups chicken stock, or enough to cover potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • salt and freshly group black pepper to taste
